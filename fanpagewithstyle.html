BIO

Alice in Chains is an American Hard Rock band from Seattle, WA. The band was founded in 1987 and their debut album, "Facelift" was released in 1990. Although they came to prominence during the infamous "Grunge" scene of the 1990's, they considered themselves a metal band at the time. The band's original lead singer La,yne Staley, passed away in 2002 after years of battling addiction problems and was eventually replaced by William DuVall in 2009.
